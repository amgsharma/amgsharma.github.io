Professional Profile

I am a Data Scientist who holds a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering. My technical background lends testimony to over 7 years utilizing critical analysis for problem solving in the context of the scientific method. My ability to communicate clearly and effectively has allowed me to hold leadership positions at various stages during my academic career. A strong sense of curiousity combined with a sophisticated analytical toolkit has driven me toward the exciting world of data science. A flexible composure and a naturally optimistic outlook renders me a competent team player and a resourceful independent worker.

Core Compentencies

Communication

I have three years of Project Management experience during which I refined the skill of transforming raw data into concise, easy to comprehend, compelling stories with captivating visuals and presenting them to non-expert collaborators who stem from different language backgrounds.

Analytical Thinking

Data driven solutions via analytical methods is an exciting concept to me. Continuously learning and refining advanced statistical methods to uncover hidden solutions or problems from large data sets no matter what field is enjoyable.

Modeling

Experienced in using historical / experimental data to create predictive models. Familiar with supervised and unsupervised machine learning techniques. Understand the underlying math that is used in the statistical methods. Able to understand core concepts and bridge learning curves quickly in order to build models for real world data in never before seen topics.
