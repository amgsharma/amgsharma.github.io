About Me

I cannot answer to where I am from. Well, no, I can, but its a much longer and meandering tale (check out the 'My Essays' link above if you're bored). I have moved so many times in my life; from my childhood paradise in Trinidad to the harsh, highly energetic and romantic little town known as 'The Big Apple', then to the fairytale land of Virginia etc etc.. and in each phase have fallen in love with the people, culture and places that I've encountered. So much so, that today the question 'where are you from' renders me in a state of panic wherein I must either lie to a perfect stranger or spend hours explaining why the question posed is not relevant to me. For my Master's project I was fortunate enough to be part of a collaborative effort with UVa, NASA and Stanford to develop a diagnostics system for scramjets. During my PhD studies, I was part of the team of aerospace engineers who designed this new hybrid airplane as part of the A.H.E.A.D program. My current interests lie in the discipline of predictive modelling and big data analytics. In transitioning to this field, I have begun to hone my skills in Python and R and have started applying statistical analytical methods to small projects which can be accessed on Github. If you are a fellow DS enthusiast feel free to find me on LinkedIn.
